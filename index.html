Who we are

Student-led • Client-focused • Engineering

Our club brings together students across engineering, design, and health disciplines to create tailored prosthetic solutions. We work closely with clients to understand their lifestyle, goals, and unique anatomy to tailor every prosthetic to their needs.

What we do: We handle the full process from design to delivery — including guiding you through measurements, 3D modeling, material selection, printing, and fitting. Each prosthetic is iterated in collaboration with the client for comfort, function, and aesthetics.

Why it matters

Custom prosthetics are often expensive and inaccessible. By combining student ingenuity with low-cost fabrication, we can serve clients who might otherwise go without.

We especially focus on, but are not limited to:

  • Clients seeking myoelectric prosthetic hands
  • Pets whose mobility impacts their quality of life
  • Individuals seeking lightweight, activity-specific devices

Interested in joining the team, becoming a client, or partnering with us? Reach out using the contact section below.

Request

Submit a prosthetic request

If you or your pet could benefit from a custom prosthetic, please submit a request. Our team will follow up via email to gather measurements, photos, and any medical or veterinary guidance. Please note that, due to our current client capacity, it may take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months to receive a reply.

  • 1
    Fill out our request form with basic contact information and a short description of the need.
  • 2
    We’ll reach out as soon as we can to ask follow-up questions and request photos or measurements.
  • 3
    Our design team will create a custom solution, share a prototype, and refine it based on your feedback.
